Why Microsoft Teams Pro?

Microsoft Teams Rooms Pro delivers an enhanced meeting experience through its intelligent audio and video, front row and large galleries, and dual screen support. With Teams Rooms Pro, your team can experience better communication and collaboration during meetings.

The Teams Rooms Pro license provides advanced management features such as remote device management, conditional access policies, and detailed device analytics. This allows for easier management and monitoring of the Teams Rooms systems and ensures the security and compliance of your organization's meetings.
